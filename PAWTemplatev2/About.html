About Pangolin Automated Watch

The Pangolin Automated Watch (PAW) - A Population Monitoring System for the Philippine Pangolin by Team Maharlika. Our goal is to monitor the pangolins in a conserved area using unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVâ€™s) that is connected to a cross platform system maintained by artificial intelligence that will keep track of trespassing poachers that will try to capture pangolins for their scales or meat. The system is made by senior high school students of Ramon Magsaysay (Cubao) High School to develop a way to help protect the Philippine pangolin form encroaching poachers in the forests of Palawan.
